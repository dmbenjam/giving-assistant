What Giving Assistant Does

Rather than a filter or keyword search, Giving Assistant uses natural language to reason across 100+ Syracuse University funds and surface the ones that genuinely align with each donor's interests and affiliations.

Donors can explore funds through personal affiliation or a cause that is important to them.

Younger generations, alumni of color, and women tend to be especially driven by passion for and connection to a specific cause, as opposed to loyalty and duty to their alma mater. Giving Assistant replaces broad annual fund appeals with personalized, values-driven discovery.

Built as a prototype for Clementine integration, Giving Assistant is designed to live inside a platform that is already slated for growth.

When donors select "Make a Gift," they're taken to a giving form prefilled with their selected fund, creating a seamless experience. For prototype purposes, this currently links to a blank form.
